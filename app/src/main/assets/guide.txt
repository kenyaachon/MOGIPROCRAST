The bar chart lists how many minutes an application has been used, the list goes in order of least used to most used. At the top of each bar, there is a number that indicates the exact amount of time the app has been used. With this chart, you are able to see your overall phone usage.

As many of the bars are quite small, you are able to zoom in to be able to view the exact amount of time the app has been used.  You can also touch each bar to get more detailed information about the specific application being displayed.
